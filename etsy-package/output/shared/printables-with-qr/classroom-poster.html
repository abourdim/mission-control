Mini Mission Control • Classroom Guide

1
📱💻

Open the Page on Two Devices

Phone, tablet, laptop — any two browsers work. Chrome is the friendliest. No app store, no login, no waiting.

2
🔤

Type the Same Room Code on Both

Agree on a secret word — pizza, alpha-42, anything. Type it exactly the same on both devices. That is the handshake!

3
🎥

Press Start Camera

Click 🎥 Start camera. When the browser asks for camera & mic, tap Allow. Your face pops into the self-view window.

4
✅

Press Connect

One of you becomes host, the other is guest. Video and audio now flow directly between you — no servers in the middle.

5
🎮

Enjoy: D-pad, Text, TTS, micro:bit

Drive the D-pad (UP / DOWN / LEFT / RIGHT / STOP). Send text messages. Hit Speak or Remote speak. Optional: pair a micro:bit V2 and the D-pad drives it over BLE.

FaceTime + Game Controller + micro:bit walkie-talkie.
